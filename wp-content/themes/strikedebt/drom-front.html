The Debt Resisters’ Operations Manual

Written by a network of activists, writers, and academics from Strike Debt, The Debt Resisters' Operations Manual maps out how the predatory debt system works to increase inequality, undermine democracy, and ruin lives. It provides detailed strategies for fighting common forms of debt and lays out an expansive vision for a societal movement of debt resistance. The full text of the manual is available here for free.
